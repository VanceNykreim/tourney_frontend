You have the power to shape the future of a volleyball player.

Every successful volleyball player has a coach or mentor who believed in them and provided unwavering support. Whether you’re a P.E. teacher, high school, club, or college volleyball coach, you are the backbone of your players’ development. We offer a wealth of resources to enhance your coaching techniques and improve your training sessions, including articles, videos, drills, and tools.

Key Resources

Southern LA Volleyball Coach Academy

The Southern LA Volleyball Coach Academy is an online, interactive platform for volleyball coaches. It offers a curated content library with learning paths, courses, and modules based on the USA Volleyball Development Model. Access a comprehensive set of resources, including articles, videos, drills, and tools, to refine your coaching strategies and enhance your training sessions.

Southern LA Volleyball Member Coach Requirements

The indoor IMPACT course is mandatory for all Southern LA Volleyball junior club coaches. It's the minimum education requirement to coach at this level. Additionally, coaches must complete SafeSport training before interacting with athletes.

Coaches Challenge

Southern LA Volleyball is part of the Million Coaches Challenge with the US Olympic & Paralympic Committee. This initiative aims to train one million coaches in youth development by 2025. Members of the Southern LA Volleyball Coach Academy can access the "Connection Based Coaching" course for free.
